Privacy Policy

1MillionDollarPhiz — The Phiz Company — Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

1MillionDollarPhiz is a private internal operations tool built exclusively for use by The Phiz Company team. It is not a public application and is not available to external users.

2. Information We Collect

The app collects the following data solely to operate its core functions:

  • Google account information — your name, email address, and profile photo, obtained via Google Sign-In.
  • Google Calendar access — if you choose to connect your Google Calendar, we store OAuth tokens to create and update calendar events on your behalf.
  • Usage data — tasks completed, revenue logged, and activity timestamps used for internal operations tracking.
  • Push notification tokens — if you enable push notifications, we store a subscription token to send you task reminders.

3. How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used exclusively for internal business operations within The Phiz Company. We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third parties outside of the services listed below.

4. Third-Party Services

5. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as the application is in use. Users may request deletion of their account data at any time by contacting the app administrator.

Access to this application is restricted to a maximum of two authorised users within The Phiz Company. No public registration is permitted.
